Hello, my name is Juweek Adolphe and I live in Brooklyn, NY. I am a 24 year old creative coder.

Ever since I was a kid, I always thought of information differently, and preferred to be taught by listening to stories. Now I'm a stroyteller; a visual journalist for the Guardian.

My project proposal is to work with scientists, politicians, and other professionals to publish visual projects about things I care about. That's kind of what I do now.

My personal project is to become stronger at visuals. It's hard to define my style because I spend so much time with code, but I enjoy drawing, and want to use this residency as an opportunity to explore an illustrative approach.

I've already started to explore what that could be. Here's a draft of an story I wrote on the future of the cities. Here are two projects I made, one an interactive piece about food, the other a data visualization about Congress.
